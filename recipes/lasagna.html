Lasagna

Description

This is a Lasagna recipe. We will need:

  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • A little bit of Amore
  • ...and all the other ingredients for Lasagna.

Steps

We cook Lasagna in the following steps

  1. Use the Salt.
  2. Use the Pepper.
  3. Add a little bit of Amore.
  4. Take the mixture, put it to the side and find a Lasagna recipe online. Then use the mixture from Steps 1 to 3 within the appropriate Lasagna recipe.
